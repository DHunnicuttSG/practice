Goblin Tower

Name: Bob

Race: Human

Class: Warrior

Health: 20/20

Gold: 20

Level: 0

Steps: 0

Potion Belt: 0

Instructions:
The game is simple, the player starts with 20 health points and will take a “step”. Each step has a chance for the player to encounter a goblin. When the player encounters a goblin. They’ll lose 2 points of health and gain 5 pieces of gold. Once the player reaches 10 steps, they have conquered a floor of our tower. After every odd floor conquered, they’ll be allowed to buy a potion for 10 gold pieces. We’ll keep the potions in a potion belt that a player can drink restoring their health by 1pt. Once the player losses all their health points, the game will be over.
